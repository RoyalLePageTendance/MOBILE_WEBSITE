About the App

The Royal LePage Tendance mobile app helps our agents stay up-to-date with the company events and the open house schedules. Integrated with Google Drive, the app helps our brokers to retrieve important documents of the company. Integrated with Google Calendar too, the app ensures every event is available to all employees. Also, our broker have access to a transaction tools via our mobile app to keep track of the remaining steps.

Key Features

  • View open house schedules
  • Google Calendar integration
  • Push notifications for new event
  • Easy access to listing details
